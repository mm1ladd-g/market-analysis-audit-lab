# Roadmap

[English documentation](docs/en/index.md) · [مستندات فارسی](docs/fa/index.md)

This roadmap describes intent, not a delivery promise. Security, rights, and evidence integrity take priority over dates.

## v0.1 — Safe public foundation (released 2026-07-15)

- Neutral configuration with no real-person defaults.
- Fully synthetic offline demo and final-bundle verification.
- Rights and API-cost gates.
- Configurable categories, asset mappings, inclusive date ranges, and Persian/English reporting.
- Structured claim extraction, conservative scoring, provenance, cache fingerprints, and SHA-256 inventories.
- Separate evidence and exact-publication checkpoints, private/public output boundaries, and a redacted reports-only web profile.
- Accessible bilingual dashboard and deterministic Persian-first/English-second PDF.
- Local-only hardened Docker default, bilingual documentation, security and governance baseline.
- Hash-locked release tooling, SBOMs, dependency-license inventory, vulnerability gates, and provenance attestations.

## v0.2 — Provider and transcript adapters

- Optional local speech-to-text pathway with word/segment timestamps.
- Additional licensed/exact provider adapters with calendar and venue metadata.
- Better data-gap detection, corporate-action handling, and symbol-map validation.
- Versioned migration tools and additional import/export conformance tests.

## v0.3 — Review and publication safety

- Claim-level review queue, correction history, and multi-reviewer policy.
- Configurable publication profiles beyond the conservative built-in public DTO.
- Relationship, sponsorship, right-of-reply, and reviewer disclosures in reports.
- Authentication/rate-limit reference deployment.

## v0.4 — Supply-chain and research maturity

- Keyless container signing, cross-platform byte-reproducibility measurements, and independent release verification.
- Expanded adversarial prompt-injection and malicious-file test corpus.
- Provider conformance suite and methodology comparison tools.
- Stable schema registry and documented extension API.

## v1.0 criteria

- Stable configuration and artifact schemas with migrations.
- Independent security and methodology review.
- Documented content-rights workflow and public-deployment privacy controls.
- Supported exact-data route for declared asset classes.
- Measured fresh-clone reproducibility on supported platforms.
- No known release-blocking security, rights, privacy, or critical correctness issue.

Requests are prioritized through public issues; paid or promotional interest does not guarantee inclusion or change scoring policy.
