# Legal and source rights

[English](legal-and-rights.md) · [فارسی](../fa/legal-and-rights.md) · [Documentation](index.md)

This guide is operational risk guidance, not legal advice.

## Content rights gate

Only ingest content you own, have permission to process, or are otherwise legally entitled to use. A public URL is not a license. The project does not grant permission to download, transcribe, store, submit to an AI provider, modify, redistribute, or publish third-party content.

YouTube's terms restrict automated access and downloading except as permitted by the service, prior permission, or applicable law. API clients have additional privacy, attribution, storage, branding, and anti-scraping obligations. Review the current [YouTube Terms](https://www.youtube.com/static?template=terms) and [YouTube API Developer Policies](https://developers.google.com/youtube/terms/developer-policies). `SOURCE_RIGHTS_ACKNOWLEDGED=true` is an operator record, not immunity or permission.

Prefer content-owner exports, rights-holder-provided captions, or an authorized API. Do not design a default audio downloader for arbitrary third-party videos. If relying on a legal exception, obtain jurisdiction-specific advice and limit copying/publication to what is necessary.

## Other rights

Market data, logos, portraits, thumbnails, fonts, and trademarks have separate terms. Record provenance, owner, retrieval date, license/permission, permitted uses, attribution, and expiry. Do not redistribute raw provider data without rights. Names and marks used for identification do not imply affiliation or endorsement.

## Financial and reputational risk

The software is research infrastructure, not investment advice, proof of profitability, analyst certification, or prediction of future performance. Avoid defamatory or misleading conclusions; preserve context, disclose uncertainty and sponsorship, offer corrections and right of reply, and have a human approve publication.

The Apache-2.0 project license covers original code/docs only, not operator-supplied evidence.
