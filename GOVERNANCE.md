# Governance

[English](GOVERNANCE.md) · [فارسی](GOVERNANCE.fa.md)

Market Analysis Audit Lab is a maintainer-led open-source project. Governance favors transparent evidence, conservative claims, user safety, and decisions that can be revisited.

## Roles

- **Users** operate the software and may open issues.
- **Contributors** submit documentation, tests, code, research, or review under the project license and DCO.
- **Reviewers** provide sustained, constructive review in an area of expertise.
- **Maintainers** merge changes, manage releases, security reports, community standards, and repository settings.

Roles are based on demonstrated work and judgment, not employment, sponsorship, nationality, or market viewpoint. Current maintainers are represented through repository permissions and the public contributor history; this neutral source release does not hard-code a personal owner list.

## Decisions

Small, reversible changes may be decided in pull-request review. Significant changes require a public issue or architecture decision record, including:

- claim or scoring semantics;
- source collection and rights behavior;
- privacy or security boundaries;
- artifact schemas and compatibility;
- data-provider trust or proxy policy;
- governance, license, or release policy.

Maintainers seek rough consensus. If consensus is unavailable, a maintainer records the decision, alternatives, evidence, risks, and a future review condition. Security embargoes and private-data incidents may be handled privately until coordinated disclosure.

## Conflicts of interest

Participants must disclose financial, employment, family, client, sponsorship, or other relationships that could reasonably affect a decision about an analyst, provider, audit, or project change. A conflicted maintainer should recuse from final approval where practical.

## Methodology integrity

No contributor may alter exclusions, denominators, examples, or presentation to favor or punish a named subject. Real audit instances are not accepted as default fixtures. Methodology changes require synthetic tests and bilingual documentation. Promotional goals never override evidence labels or limitations.

## Maintainer changes

Maintainers may nominate contributors with sustained, trustworthy participation. Inactive maintainers may step down or be moved to emeritus status after reasonable private contact. Repository administrators may remove access promptly for compromised accounts, serious conduct violations, or security risk and document the action when safe.

## Amendments

Governance changes use the same public review process as other significant changes and are recorded in the changelog. This document creates project practice, not an employment relationship, partnership, or guaranteed service obligation.
